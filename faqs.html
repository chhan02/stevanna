frequently asked questions

we're not being mean, we're just very particular

When do I need to RSVP by?

Please send in your RSVP via mail before Oct 15, 2022. There will be stamps and Misa seals provided with the RSVP.

Can I bring my kids?

We would prefer the kids to stay at home. Please find a babysitter. Direct family is an exception. Please contact us if you have further questions.

If you don't know if you're family, please call us. Seriously, we need to talk about that.

Can I bring a +1?

No. The names on your invitation indicates how many guests. Do not add extra names.

Is it okay to take photos during the wedding?

Yes! But be respectful. 

At church, do not block the center aisle or wander around during the ceremony.

Our photographer has a great crew. They will be able to capture great moments for us.

Save all your photos and videos. We would love for you to share with us.

What is church etiquette? 

Church is a holy space for many of us. Please be respectful. Silence your phones. Do not talk loudly. Do not wander during the ceremony. Do not block the aisle.

Absolutely no live streaming, Facebook Live, Instagram Live, etc.

Can everyone attend the traditional ceremonies?

This is mainly for family. Please contact us if you have questions.

Where can I stay?

There are many hotels and Airbnb's in the area. The church and venue addresses will be provided with your invitation. Please search accordingly.
